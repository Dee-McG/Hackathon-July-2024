Where To Donate?

If you have enjoyed learning the terms, concepts and flags around LGBTQIA++ then consider donating to one of the below charities that help progress the cause.

Stonewall

Over the last 30 years, Stonewall have helped create transformative change in the lives of LGBTQ+ people in the UK. Today, they have equal rights to love, marry and have children, and our lives, families and relationships are represented as part of the national curriculum in most of the UK.

Donate
Fighting With Pride

Fighting With Pride (FWP) was created on the 20th anniversary of the complete lifting of the ban on LGBT+ service – the ‘gay ban’. They are here to support LGBT+ Veterans, serving personnel and their families, particularly those who were affected by the ‘gay ban’.

Donate
LGBT Foundation

The LGBT Foundation is a touchstone for the LGBT community to get the help they need without fear, judgment or discrimination. When people access any of their services they know that there will be a friendly, welcoming and accepting person to support them through the whole process.
